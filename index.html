Security Protocol

How BioLabs protects your data and privacy

Data Encryption

All data transmitted between users and BioLabs servers is encrypted using industry-standard TLS 1.3 (256-bit). Profile data, credentials, and personal information are encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption on our Supabase backend.

Manual Verification Process

BioLabs employs a 3-step manual verification protocol for all mentor and researcher profiles: (1) Document submission, (2) Institutional email confirmation, (3) Admin review and approval. This ensures zero fake credentials on the platform.

Data Storage & Residency

All user data is stored on servers located within India, in compliance with DPDP Act 2023 (India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act). We do not transfer data internationally without explicit user consent.

Privacy Controls

Users have full control over their profile visibility. You can set your profile to public, connections-only, or private at any time from your dashboard settings. BioLabs never sells user data to third parties.

Responsible Disclosure

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to security@biolabs.in. We have a bug bounty program and will acknowledge valid submissions within 48 hours.

UGC Guidelines Sync

How BioLabs aligns with University Grants Commission policies

UGC-Compliant Research Platform

BioLabs Research operates in full compliance with University Grants Commission (UGC) norms and the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 framework.

Academic Research Recognition

BioLabs project opportunities and showcase publications are aligned with UGC research credit norms. Students can claim academic credits for verified project work completed through the platform, subject to their institution's policies.

Anti-Plagiarism Standards

All research content submitted to BioLabs must meet UGC anti-plagiarism guidelines. We recommend and provide access to iThenticate/Turnitin similarity checks for manuscript submissions. Plagiarised content is removed immediately.

Mentor Qualification Standards

All mentors on BioLabs must hold a minimum of a PhD or equivalent qualification from a UGC/AICTE recognized institution, or have 10+ years of industry experience. This aligns with UGC qualified teacher regulations.

NEP 2020 Alignment

BioLabs supports NEP 2020's vision of multidisciplinary research by enabling cross-domain collaborations between engineering, medical, psychology, and biotechnology domains within a single platform ecosystem.
